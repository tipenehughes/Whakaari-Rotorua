About us

Bringing Māori Soul to the Heart of Europe

Whakaari Rotorua are a group of performers from Rotorua, New Zealand, who travel around the world sharing their Māori culture through traditional song and dance. They specifically travel to the Czech Republic often as they have a deep connection to this country – especially group leader Frank Grapl Junior, as his father, František Grapl, emigrated to New Zealand in 1950.
